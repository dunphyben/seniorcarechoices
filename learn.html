Absolute Peace of Mind

Finding a Senior Living Community or Care facility for your loved ones is no easy task. We're here to make this process a lot easier, at no charge to you.

Your loved ones may have special needs that not all facilities can meet. You may have lots of questions, like how much will it cost? How close is it to home? How skilled and trustworthy are the staff?

At Senior Care Choices, our goal is to provide you with professional support to make the right decision. We make the process of selecting the right Senior Living facility for your loved ones as simple as 1, 2, 3.

1. Chat with us

After filling out our initial questionnaire, we meet to learn more about your situation. Each family's situation is different. This meeting ensures that we understand fully your needs, desires, and budget.

2. Community Selection

Based on the information learned from our initial meeting, we provide you with a list of homes that best serve your needs and budget.

3. Decision and Move-in

We will help you every step of the way until and beyond your loved ones have moved in to their new home. Whether you need logistics assistance or scheduling help, we are there with you every step of the way.

Ready to get started?

Complete our brief questionnaire and a Senior Care Choices professional will be in touch.
